The Injector type Stimulant is an enhanced design used by ARK, the military police force for riot and emergency situations where a user needs to expend their stamina in a rush instantly. The effects are instantly noticeable however will leave the user fatigued for a short duration and will not do anything if the user is already in a hasted state, meaning users must be smart about their usage.

    additional notes:

  1. needs to be consumed on low dose
  2. use Raw SPICE to counter fatal effects
  3. the effect is short so be careful
  4. you may close your nose from the smell
